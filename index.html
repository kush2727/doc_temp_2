Trained at Johns Hopkins School of Medicine and completing fellowship at Mayo Clinic, Dr. Mercer specializes in advanced diagnostics, preventive cardiology, and minimally invasive intervention. He believes that the best medicine begins with listening.

What I Offer

Specialized Services

Comprehensive cardiac and internal medicine services tailored to each patient's unique needs.

Preventive Cardiology

Risk assessment, lipid management, lifestyle counseling, and early intervention to keep your heart performing at its best.

Wellness

Electrocardiography

Advanced ECG, Holter monitoring, and event recording to detect arrhythmias, conduction defects, and ischemic changes.

Diagnostics

Echocardiography

State-of-the-art ultrasound imaging to evaluate heart structure, valve function, and wall motion abnormalities.

Imaging

Hypertension Management

Personalized blood pressure programs combining medication, nutrition, and monitoring to protect heart and kidney health.

Chronic Care

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Structured recovery programs post-heart attack or surgery, blending exercise physiology, nutrition, and psychological support.

Recovery

Telehealth Consultations

Secure, convenient virtual visits for follow-ups, prescription renewals, and second opinions from the comfort of your home.
